About the Village

Nesbal is a picturesque village located in Bandipora district, Jammu and Kashmir, India. Nestled on the serene banks of Mansbal Lake, this village is a treasure of natural beauty. With lush green surroundings, gentle hills, and fresh air, it is a peaceful haven.

The village is home to a number of educational institutions and a mosque that reflects its cultural and spiritual harmony. Its proximity to Mansbal Lake and the surrounding hills makes it a scenic and tranquil destination.
